ABOUT ME



Hello! My name is Dan Narcis Costinel, and I am a second-year student at the FACULTY OF AUTOMATIC CONTROL AND COMPUTER SCIENCE, part of the National University of Science and Technology Bucharest, specializing in SYSTEMS ENGINEERING.


Since middle school, I have developed a passion for mathematics, being drawn to the fact that solving problems required ANALYTICAL THINKING and a bit of creativity. I have always liked leaving my mark on what I do, so in high school, I chose the MATHEMATICS-INFORMATICS profile, where I discovered that I enjoy computer science as it helps me further develop my ANALYTICAL THINKING IN RESOLVING COMPLEX PROBLEMS.


In 12th grade, I started questioning myself: What faculty do I want to attend?, What would I like to do in the future?, If I were to continue with mathematics and computer science, what options do I have?. I decided to pursue the field of automation because I want to learn about their APPLICATION in everyday life, and the field of systems engineering allows me to achieve this.


My studies in the field of AUTOMATION and the subjects I have taken so far have given me a deeper understanding of this vast domain, confirming that I made the RIGHT CHOICE by enrolling in this faculty. I aspire to develop my career in this field, contributing to innovative and efficient solutions in the future.
